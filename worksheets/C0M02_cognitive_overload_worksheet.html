Part I

Part II

Classify Your Overload

For each overload type, identify your specific symptoms:

Production Overload: What have you produced that nobody has reviewed, deployed, or used yet?
Context Overload: What connections, dependencies, or decisions are only in YOUR head right now?
Identity Overload: Where are you the bottleneck because only you can verify the voice/quality?
Part III

The Absorption Test

⏱ This Week

Exercise: Measure Your True Cycle Time

Pick one project. Produce a batch of work using AI. Send it to your team or collaborator. Then measure:

What did you produce? How long did it take you?
How long did it take your team/collaborator to process it?
What's the ratio? (Your production time : Their processing time)

"If you don't have a whole team who can take the output at the same pace as you produce it, then it actually just creates a lot of confusion."

— Dr. Yamicia Connor
