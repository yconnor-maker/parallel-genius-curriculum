Part III

Strengths vs. Failure Modes

For each phase of knowledge work, rate how well AI performs and where it fails for YOUR specific work:

Phase 1 — Research & Brainstorming: How useful is AI for your research? Give a specific example.
Phase 2 — Structuring & Framework: When has AI given you a framework that saved time? When has a framework been wrong or misleading?
Phase 3 — Execution: Where does AI fail in your workflow? What's YOUR "last 30%"?
Part IV

The Confidence Audit

⏱ 10 Minutes

Exercise: Find the Errors

Ask an AI a detailed question in your area of expertise. Read the response carefully. Document what you find:

What question did you ask?
How many things were factually correct?
How many things sounded correct but were wrong or misleadingly simplified?
Would a non-expert have caught those errors? Why or why not?
