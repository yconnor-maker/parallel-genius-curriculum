Module 07

The Production Loop

Idea to Deliverable — The 70% Gap

Track: ResearchDuration: 90 minFormat: Lecture + Workshop

Overview

Core Thesis

AI generates thinking. Humans do the making. And the making hasn't gotten faster — there's just vastly more of it. Understanding this loop is the difference between productivity and burnout.

"AI gets you seventy percent of the way there. But the last thirty percent — the part that turns a draft into a finished product — is where all the actual work lives. And now there's ten times more of that last thirty percent than there used to be."

Key Concepts

What You'll Learn

1. Three Phases of Knowledge Work

Research & Design (AI accelerates 10x) → Refinement (dictation method matters most here) → Execution (where AI falls apart and the real work lives).

2. The Production Loop

INGEST (get everything into the system as drafts) → COMPLETE (push through the completion workflow) → DISTRIBUTE (template + deploy + promote).

3. Clinical Work as Content Substrate

One bedside dictation generated a 6-episode teaching series, 9 clinical atomic units, and full EMR notes. The clinical work IS the content substrate.

4. 70 Articles, 2 Afternoons

"I completed seventy articles in two afternoons using Claude's Cowork feature. My team couldn't absorb it. They're still working through the backlog."
