03
Framework

Three Types of AI-Accelerated Overload

1

Production Overload

You produce faster than anyone can review, approve, or deploy. 70 articles sit in a queue. The work exists but doesn't move.

2

Context Overload

YOU become the central nervous system. The AI doesn't remember across sessions. Your team doesn't have your full picture. You carry every connection in your head.

3

Identity Overload

When AI produces text that sounds like you, only you can verify the voice. You become the quality bottleneck.

The Paradox

The Super Productivity Paradox

The better you get at using AI, the more cognitive load you accumulate.

A beginner: one task at a time. Low cognitive load.

An expert: 8 agents, 12 projects in parallel, dependencies between workstreams, context no single session can hold.

The expert is 10× more productive — and 10× more cognitively loaded.
