Diagram 5 · Module 7
The Production Loop
Content production at The Labora Collective is a continuous loop. It never finishes — it just keeps cycling.
Ingest
Get everything into Coda as draft rows. Whatever state it's in, just get it registered. If it's not in Coda, it doesn't exist.
STAGE 1
Complete
Push drafts through the Article Completion Workflow: anchor image, actual text (proofread, stylized), references, social derivatives, sensitivity review, HTML build.
STAGE 2
Distribute
HTML templating → Netlify deploy. Selected articles → LinkedIn + Substack. Weekly newsletter assembly + social promotion.
