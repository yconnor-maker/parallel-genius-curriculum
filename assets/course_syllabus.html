Course Overview
Not a Course About Prompting

This curriculum teaches the real skills behind effective AI use — skills that were learned in operating rooms, not tech blogs. Every concept comes from actual production systems, real operational failures, and over a year of daily intensive AI use.

"I don't use prompts. I talk to the AI the way I talk to a colleague. I explain what I'm trying to do. I think out loud. I contradict myself. I explore tangents. I come back to the main thread."
